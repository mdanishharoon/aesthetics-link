## Design Context

### Users
This storefront serves both retail customers and clinic/B2B buyers. The thank-you page appears after checkout completion and needs to reassure both audiences that the order is confirmed, while giving them the details they need to continue confidently without checking email first.

### Brand Personality
Premium, professional, assured. The interface should feel polished and trustworthy rather than playful or casual, with enough substance to support B2B purchasing expectations.

### Aesthetic Direction
Premium B2B/professional with clear transactional structure. The design should feel composed, elevated, and information-rich, with strong hierarchy and full order details visible on-page. Avoid bland placeholder success-page patterns and avoid generic startup-celebration UI.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with confidence: important transactional confirmations must feel decisive and trustworthy.
2. Show the substance: critical order details should be visible on the page, not deferred to email.
3. Respect the hierarchy: structure complex information into clear sections with one dominant focal point.
4. Design for both retail and clinic buyers: professional enough for B2B, clear enough for general consumers.
5. Success states should feel complete: the end of checkout should feel like a polished handoff, not an empty dead-end.
